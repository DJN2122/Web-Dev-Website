About Us

John's Workshop was founded by John in 2018. It is located in Blanchardstown, a suburban area in Dublin 15, 20km from Dublin city centre. We supply Salvage and Architectural Reclamation, though we specialise in Reclaimed Timber Beams,Timber & Tile Flooring, Belfast Sinks, Bricks, Slates and Sleepers. John has a lot of experience in areas like carpentry, engineering and architecture.

We have supplied many Bars and Restaurants with our products including all across Ireland who will testify to our quality and price

Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.
