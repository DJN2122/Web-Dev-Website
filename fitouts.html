Our Fitouts

We pride ourselves in our fitout service. We have provided salvaged materials to a number of bars and restaurants around Ireland. See below some of our work and contact us for further information.


×

Davey Mac's, Waterford

Waterford’s Newest Oldest Bar, Davey Macs is a Fantastic Hidden Gem in the Heart of Waterford City with a Fabulous & Unique Interior Décor. We installed a salvaged oak bar and dividers taken from an 18th century pub. This fitout was one of our most detailed and popular work to date.


×

Milano, Blanchardstown

Milano, Blanchardstown has been serving Blanchardstown and surrounding areas since 2018. We were delighted to install a small wooden outdoor dining area which was constructed using salvaged oal, and 10cm x 30cm timber joists for roof structure providing shelter for customers dining outside.

This fitout was completed in the summer of 2019 and it took approximately 2 weeks to finish. 75% of the timber is recycled and reused from different projects. We always try our best to save and help the environment the best we can.
